Example Project

Movie Webpage Project

Context

After learning HTML and CSS, me and my partner had to create a movie project. This movie provided us alot of freedom, although alot of rules and aspects to follow. Me and my partner named our movie "The Twin Heist"

Content

Our Project was about two twins who are opposites, one was good and one was evil. The twins goes after an art piece and at the end one twin dies. There were both HTML and CSS parts that had to be completed to get full credit. For example, we had to come up with a plan so me and my partner would know what we were going to do seperately which also included each doing the colors, fonts, class and Id's, margins and paddings,etc.

Reflection

Some challenges we faced were communications and centering the images. We both didn't know how to make the pictures center, but after going through the padding and margins on freecodecamp, i tried it and it worked. Additionally, communication was hard because we bpth didn't know each other well. This led to us not knowing what idea we would start on, also if one of us were confused on one part we couldn't really get pass that problem.
